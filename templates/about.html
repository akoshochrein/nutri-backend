About

What is Nutri?

Nutri is a chat bot that tries to help you with nutrition facts about the fruits and vegetables that you are unsure of. Be aware, that Nutri is not always correct. And is never 100% sure about the guessed food.

What does Nutri know about you?

It might seem like that Nutri knows a lot of things about your nutritient facts and uses this for evil doings in a science lab, however, Nutri does not store any of your private data, including your email address.

Who to contact with any questions?

That would be the creator of Nutri, Akos Hochrein.
